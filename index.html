About Us

Enwa Hardware Ltd business store is located along Etwop-Bala road, Anai Ober village between Omodo and Orlando trading centers. The hardware is positioned where materials are easily accessible to the community. In 2022 we started a project of constructing a storey building but finding building materials to feed our project became a dilemma. All the materials were brought in from Lira town and some from Kampala. At that point we realized that we needed to bring the services nearer to the people. The purpose of-this business is to be nearer to the residents of Omodo and Anai Ober who are just starting to build their new homes and to supply them with the products they need with guaranteed quality. A hardware store is one of the top 5 things needed in the community of Anai Ober and surrounding areas. Our research also disclosed that hardware stores in surrounding areas are limited with their supply and they offer building materials that are not of good quality. So we started Enwa to address those challenges. Enwa has convenient opening and delivery hours which runs from 7:00am -6:30pm. To ease the pressure of transport Enwa has a truck that delivers materials to customers who make bulk purchases and offer a discount transport fare to all our other customers. Enwa hardware was brought closer to help the community realize their building dreams. There for we argue the community to build with confidence.
